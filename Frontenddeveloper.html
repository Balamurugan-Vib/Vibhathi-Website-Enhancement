Job Description

We are seeking a talented and passionate Frontend Developer to join our team and help create dynamic, responsive, and user-friendly web applications. As a Frontend Developer, you will collaborate with designers, backend developers, and other stakeholders to bring user interface designs to life, ensuring seamless functionality, performance, and an exceptional user experience.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead the Technical architecture and development activities for various COTS and inhouse applications
  • Design & architecture for the solutions, in line with the technology roadmap agreed with client and Business teams.
  • Working with partners and /or internal engineering teams for building & testing solutions
  • Working with business leads for vendor identification, preparing product requirement document, agreeing project plan & timelines, commercials & success criteria.
  • Build & train team of developers and designers needed to implement the PoCs, MVPs and solutions and taking accountability for in-time and quality delivery.
  • Modifying the Technical design / code basis test result and inputs received from business teams of client.
  • Coordinating with vendors to install necessary hardware at site, integrating applications with airport systems, as required.
  • Once Solution is complete to client satisfaction, work with heads towards productization the into a marketable solution which can be offered to existing and potential clients.
  • Provide technicalsupport to Databricks users, including troubleshooting technical issues, analyzing logs, and resolving problems promptly.
  • Partnership with vendors and OEMs for sourcing the key product hardware and software.
  • Bundling the product with other products or services to be offered as a complete solution.
  • Developing necessary in-house capability such as infrastructure, network & communication, data centre, applications and use-cases.
  • Providing installation, system integration & training services at the time of deployment at client location
  • Creating and maintaining knowledge database to be used by the team.
  • Preparing & disseminating training material for the design and development team
  • Tracking, monitoring, and reporting regular updates to the management.

Qualifications and Experience

  • Bachelor’s degree in computer science, human-computer interaction, interaction design, or a related field.
  • 7+ years recent experience in Frontend Development specifically with React.js,Typescript
  • Strong proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Familiarity with state management libraries (e.g., Redux, Context API).
  • Familiarity with testing frameworks (e.g., Jest, Cypress).
  • Understanding of RESTful APIs and asynchronous request handling.
  • Knowledge of modern build tools (e.g., Webpack, Babel).
  • Experience with version control systems, preferably Git.
  • Knowledge of web performance optimization techniques.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

Technical Skills:

    • React.js, Typescript, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Redux, Jest
