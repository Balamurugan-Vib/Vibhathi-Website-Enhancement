Job Description

We are looking for an experienced Enterprise Cloud Datacenter Architect to lead the design, deployment, and optimization of our cloud and datacenter infrastructure. As a key technical expert, you will be responsible for creating scalable, secure, and efficient solutions that meet business objectives while leveraging cutting-edge cloud and datacenter technologies. This role requires a strong understanding of hybrid cloud environments, virtualization, and advanced datacenter operations.

IT Infrastructure support:

  • Preparing datacenter solution design & driving end to end technical Implementation.
  • Should be able to create and drive on Cloud strategy.
  • L4 Support for providing resolution to very critical /Airport outage IT infra incidents and problems.
  • Help local teams in RCA and Corrective/preventive actions preparations and submit to management.
  • Standardization of data center operations by proposing common monitoring tools and SOPs & Driving the implementation.
  • Coordination with SDHs for improving IT infra Support.

Availability management:

  • Driving the Implementation of centralized monitoring tools.
  • Common monitoring parameters and thresholds across all the airports.

Capacity management:

  • Responsible to do Analysis of resources utilization –Airport wise & propose additional capacities..
  • Responsible to prepare Capacity management plan-Airport wise.
  • Driving additional hardware/software/solutions implementation to meet business requirements.

Tech Refresh project and new initiatives:

  • Technical SPOC for CDC-Virtualization & Cloud Implementation - Servers/Storage - Revamping Tracking actionable items and ensuring completing milestones as per project schedule
  • Coordinating with vertical leads, Consultants & Vendors and Conducting Review meetings.
  • Coordinating with concern teams to provide the required information to completion of the projects in-time

New/Adhoc projects:

  • New initiatives for operational /business improvements based on data analysis.
  • Extending support for innovative initiatives, Airport digitization projects.
  • Preparing SOW, RFP Documentation, and technical evaluations.
  • Design and implementation support.
  • New projects take over support.

Team Management:

  • Work closely with the team and conduct monthly reviewing meetings to fill the gaps.
  • Drive the team development initiative in alignment with IT Operations.
  • Engage with the team members to motivate and foster teamwork, understand, and address concerns and provide a collaborative environment for them to develop.
  • Ensure KT among team members.

Educational qualifications:

  • Post Graduate Degree/Graduate/Diploma (Diploma candidates requires additional 4 years of experience)

Certifications (minimum one is mandatory)

  • AWS certified solution architect-professional
  • Google Cloud solution architect
  • VMware certified professional-CMA Cloud management automation or DCV.
  • MCSE: Cloud Platform and Infrastructure certification
  • Google professional cloud architect

Relevant & total years of experience:

  • 15+ years of total experience in the field of IT solutions and preferred in Airport IT.
