Job Description

We are looking for a highly skilled and visionary Network Architect to design, implement, and oversee the company’s complex network infrastructure. In this role, you will be responsible for creating scalable, secure, and reliable network solutions that meet the demands of our organization and clients. Your expertise will drive the optimization of our IT environment while ensuring seamless connectivity, performance, and security across all platforms.

Key Responsibilities:

    Planning and Designing Networks:

    • Network architects will meticulously plan and design data communication networks. These networks can range from smaller local area networks (LANs) to larger wide area networks (WANs), L3 MPLS Networks and Campus wide Optical Fiber based L2 & l3 Networks.
    • They collaborate with management staff to present network plans and ensure alignment with the organization's business goals.

    Overseeing Implementation:

    • During the network implementation process, network architects set schedules, allocate budgets, and manage the deployment of routers, cables, modems, and other essential hardware and software.
    • They ensure that the infrastructure operates effectively and efficiently.

    Information and Network Security:

    • Network architects play a crucial role in maintaining the security of data communication networks. They implement security protocols, firewalls, and encryption measures to safeguard sensitive information.

    Upgrades and Maintenance:

    • As technology evolves, network architects stay up-to-date by researching network advancements. They proactively implement upgrades to both hardware and software components as needed.
    • Regular maintenance and repairs to network equipment are also part of their responsibilities.

    Documentation and Communication:

    • Network architects maintain comprehensive documentation of networks, including drawings, descriptions, and configurations.
    • They communicate with management, IT specialists, and other team members to ensure seamless network operations.
