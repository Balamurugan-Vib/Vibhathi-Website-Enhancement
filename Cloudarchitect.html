Job Description

We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Cloud Architect with expertise in the airport domain to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in cloud architecture, Solution, individual contributors, and hands-on experience with both AWS and Azure platforms.

Key Responsibilities:

    Cloud Solution and Architecture Design:

    • Architect, design, and implement scalable and secure cloud solutions for airport related applications.
    • Lead the implementation of cloud solutions, ensuring adherence to best practices, Compliance requirement and security standards.
    • Troubleshoot and resolve issues related to cloud infrastructure and services.

    Hands-on Experience:

    • Demonstrate hands-on expertise in implementing cloud solutions using AWS and Azure.
    • Troubleshoot and resolve issues related to cloud infrastructure and applications.

    Airport Domain Knowledge:

    • Possess in-depth knowledge of the airport domain, including relevant technologies and industry standards.
    • Understand the unique challenges and requirements of airport systems.

    Security and Compliance:

    • Implement and enforce security measures to protect cloud-based assets.
    • Ensure compliance with industry standards and regulations.

    Performance Optimization:

    • Optimize the performance of cloud-based applications and infrastructure.
    • Conduct regular performance reviews and implement improvements.

    Collaboration:

    • Work closely with stakeholders to understand business requirements and translate them into effective cloud solutions.
    • Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including developers, system administrators, and security experts.

    Multi-Cloud Expertise:

    • Proficient in working with both AWS and Azure platforms.
    • Design and implement solutions that leverage the strengths of each cloud provider.

    Continuous Improvement:

    • Drive continuous improvement initiatives in cloud architecture, ensuring scalability, reliability, and performance.
    • Ensure compliance with industry standards and regulations.

    Cost Management:

    • Develop cost-effective solutions and strategies for optimizing cloud expenses.
    • Monitor and manage cloud resource usage to control costs.

Qualifications and Experience

  • Bachelor’s or master’s degree in computer science, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • Proven experience as a Cloud Architect, with a focus on individual contributors and hands-on implementation.
  • Minimum of 12 years of experience in IT, with at least 8 years in a Cloud Architect role.
  • Extensive experience with AWS and Azure cloud platforms
  • In-depth knowledge of AWS and Azure services, including but not limited to EC2, S3, Lambda, Azure Virtual Machines, Azure Blob Storage, etc.
  • Strong understanding of cloud security, compliance, and best practices.
  • Proven experience in designing and implementing scalable and highly available cloud solutions.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Strong expertise in AWS and Azure services, with relevant certifications.
  • Relevant certifications such as AWS Certified Solutions Architect, Microsoft Certified: Azure Solutions Architect Expert.
  • Familiarity with security best practices in cloud environments.
